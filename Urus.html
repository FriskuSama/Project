Lamborghini Urus

  • release date:2017
  • Assembly location:Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy
  • Engine power: 4.0-liter V8 engine(641 hp)

About:

The Lamborghini Urus is a high-performance SUV (sport utility vehicle) produced by the Italian automaker Lamborghini. It was first introduced in 2017 and represents the brand's entry into the SUV market.

The Urus features a front-engine layout and is powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine, which produces 641 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. It is available with an eight-speed automatic transmission and Lamborghini's all-wheel-drive system.

Despite its size and weight, the Urus is surprisingly agile and fast, with a top speed of 190 mph and a 0 to 60 mph time of around 3.5 seconds. It also has a sophisticated suspension system and advanced driving aids, which help to improve its handling and performance on the road.

The Urus's exterior design is characterized by sharp angles, aggressive styling, and a muscular profile that is typical of Lamborghini's sports cars. The interior is spacious and luxurious, with high-quality materials and a wide range of features and amenities.

The Urus is designed to be a high-performance SUV that can be used for a wide range of purposes, from daily commuting to off-road adventures. It is also intended to appeal to a wider range of customers, including families and those who need a more practical and versatile car.

Overall, the Lamborghini Urus is a unique and impressive SUV that combines Lamborghini's high-performance DNA with the practicality and versatility of an SUV. It has been well-received by critics and customers alike and is considered to be a significant milestone in Lamborghini's history.
