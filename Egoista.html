Lamborghini Egoista

  • release date:2013
  • Assembly location:Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy
  • Engine power:5.7-liter V12 engine(between 485 and 595 hp)

About:

The Lamborghini Egoista is a concept car that was unveiled in 2013 to celebrate Lamborghini's 50th anniversary. It is a single-seat, high-performance supercar that was designed to be a pure expression of Lamborghini's extreme and uncompromising design philosophy.

The Egoista features a mid-engine layout and is powered by a 5.2-liter V10 engine, which produces 600 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque. It is mated to a six-speed automatic transmission and Lamborghini's all-wheel-drive system.

The Egoista's exterior design is characterized by sharp angles, aggressive styling, and aerodynamic features that are inspired by the Apache helicopter. The body is made entirely of carbon fiber, and the cockpit canopy can be removed to give the driver a full panoramic view.

The Egoista's interior is also designed to be futuristic and high-tech, with a driver-focused layout and a jet fighter-style steering wheel. The cockpit is trimmed in aluminum and carbon fiber, and features a single racing-style seat.

The Lamborghini Egoista was designed to be a concept car, and only one unit was produced, which is now owned by Lamborghini and kept on display at the Lamborghini Museum in Italy. It represents Lamborghini's extreme and uncompromising approach to automotive design and engineering, and serves as a showcase for the brand's cutting-edge technology and innovation.
